About Tastiera

Bridging Kitchen & Customer

Empowering Egyptian home cooks through technology, trust, and community

Our Mission

Tastiera was born from a simple observation: Egyptian home cooks create incredible food, but struggle to connect with customers in the digital age. Our platform uses AI to solve three critical challenges — digital literacy gaps, trust deficits, and payment barriers — that hold back thousands of talented home cooks.

By acting as a Matcher, Automator, Analyzer, and Recommender, our AI system creates a seamless bridge between home kitchens and hungry customers. We verify sellers, automate order management, analyze food quality, and personalize recommendations — all while respecting the cultural heritage of Egyptian home cooking.
